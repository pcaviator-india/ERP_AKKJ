HOW TO GET YOUR REAL CERTIFICATES:
===================================

1. Open QZ Tray (desktop application)
2. Click Settings (gear icon in top-right)
3. Go to "Certificates" tab
4. Click "Export" button
5. Copy the Certificate text (starts with -----BEGIN CERTIFICATE-----)
6. Copy the Private Key text (starts with -----BEGIN PRIVATE KEY-----)
7. Paste them into Accessories Settings > QZ Tray section

These demo certs are for reference only. Use your actual certificates from QZ Tray.
